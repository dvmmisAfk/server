Who We Are

LaundryLux is a laundry service that started in 2012. We take care of your washing, drying and ironing. We started from one small shop and now serve over 5000 customers every month across the city.

What We Do

We pick up your dirty clothes from your house, wash them with good quality detergents, and deliver them back clean and neatly folded. We also do dry cleaning. All clothes are handled with care.

Why Choose Us

- Free pickup and delivery
- Affordable prices
- No damage to clothes
- On time service
- Eco-friendly detergents
