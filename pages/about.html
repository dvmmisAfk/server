Who We Are

LaundryLux has been serving the community since 2012. We started as a small neighborhood laundromat and have grown into a full-service laundry and dry-cleaning business trusted by over 10,000 customers. Our commitment is simple — return every garment cleaner, fresher, and better than we received it.

Our Mission

We believe clean clothes shouldn't be a chore. By combining professional-grade equipment with eco-friendly detergents and free pickup/delivery, we make laundry effortless for busy families and professionals alike.
